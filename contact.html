Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Koru Impact

How long does a typical engagement last?

Engagement duration varies based on project scope. Research studies typically take 3-6 months, well-being programs can range from 6-12 months, and comprehensive organizational transformation projects may extend to 18-24 months.

Do you work with organizations of all sizes?

Yes, we work with startups, mid-size companies, and large enterprises. We tailor our approach to meet the unique needs and resources of each organization.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have experience across various sectors including education, healthcare, technology, and non-profits. Our evidence-based approach is particularly effective in organizations with strong Turkish cultural contexts.

How do you measure success?

We establish clear KPIs at the beginning of each engagement, including both business metrics and well-being impact measures. Our research-based approach ensures quantifiable results and regular reporting for transparency.
