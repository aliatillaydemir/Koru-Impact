About Koru Impact

Your Cultural Sherpa guiding organizations through the complex terrain of sustainable human and organizational flourishing.

Our Mission

To serve as a catalyst for sustainable human and organizational flourishing. We achieve this by building a synergistic ecosystem where rigorous social science research, evidence-based well-being practices, and purpose-driven consulting converge to empower individuals and organizations to reach their full potential and create a lasting, positive legacy.

Koru Impact is a multi-disciplinary strategic consulting firm that operates at the intersection of social science, organizational psychology, and sustainable development. Our unique value proposition lies in our integrated, four-pillar ecosystem that begins with rigorous research and culminates in transformative social impact.

At our core, we are a "Cultural Sherpa" for our clients, guiding them through the complex terrain of organizational change and making the process of building a healthier, more impactful company feel supported, clear, and effortless.

Our Values

Systemic Empathy

Meet Our Founder

Şener Cem Irmak

Founder & CEO, Koru Impact

Şener Cem Irmak is a psychologist, systemic counsellor, and the founder of Koru Impact. He is dedicated to promoting mental health and driving organizational flourishing through evidence-based consulting and systemic empathy.

With a unique academic background that combines a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering with a Bachelor's in Psychology from Bilkent University, a Master's in Neurobiology and Behavior from Universität Bielefeld, and a Master's in Marriage and Family Therapy/Counseling from Istanbul Medeniyet University, Şener brings a rare, multi-disciplinary perspective to the challenges of the modern workplace.

His professional experience includes roles as a Well-being Specialist for global firms like Zevo Health, a counselor for the British International Schools of Istanbul, and a Provisional Psychologist for the Turkish Armed Forces. As the founder of Koru Impact, Şener now focuses on integrating his expertise in psychology, technology, and systems thinking to help organizations build healthier, more resilient, and more impactful cultures.
